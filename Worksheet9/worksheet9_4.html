Task:

Compare projection shadows to shadow mapping by listing advantages anddisadvantages of the two techniques

Answer:

    Projection Shadows

    • Advantages:
      • Simple Implementation
      • Accurate Silhouette Shadows
      • Minimal Overdraw
    • Disadvantages:
      • Limited to Silhouette Shadows
      • High Computational Cost
      • Geometry Dependent

    Shadow Mapping

    • Advantages:
      • Flexible Technique
      • Efficient Rendering
      • Supports Dynamic Objects
    • Disadvantages:
      • Aliasing Artifacts
      • Depth Biasing Required
      • Complexity with Transparent Objects
